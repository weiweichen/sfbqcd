Circle Left

Circle Left is a gender neutral contra dance in Oakland
on the second Friday of the month.

First Unitarian Church of Oakland
685 14th Street
(only a few blocks from 12th St BART)

Introductory lesson: 7:30 - 8 p.m.
Dance: 8 - 11 p.m.

$5 for ages 30 and under
$10 general admission
Or pay what you can. Everyone's welcome!
Cash only, please!
